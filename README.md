## Overview

This repository showcases a cybersecurity consulting engagement conducted for **Safempire Inc.**, a technology startup preparing to launch its new social networking platform, **SafeConnect**.

Following a review of the application's business objectives and security requirements, I was engaged as the lead cybersecurity consultant to assess the organization's cyber risk exposure and provide data-driven recommendations for reducing business risk.

The engagement combines qualitative risk analysis, quantitative financial modeling, and executive-level decision support to determine whether proposed security investments are financially justified.



## Business Scenario

Safempire Inc. is preparing to launch **SafeConnect**, a mobile social networking application that enables users to:

- Share photos and videos
- Publish personal updates
- Interact with other users
- Purchase premium features through in-app payments

Because the application will process sensitive user information and financial transactions, executive leadership requested a comprehensive cybersecurity risk assessment before launch.



## Consulting Objectives

The engagement focused on helping executive leadership understand:

- The organization's current cyber risk exposure
- Critical business assets requiring protection
- Major threats affecting the application
- Technical and operational vulnerabilities
- Financial impact of cyber incidents
- Whether proposed security investments provide measurable business value



## Assessment Scope

The assessment covered:

- User database
- Payment processing system
- Backend infrastructure
- Application source code
- Authentication systems
- Supporting business processes



## Assessment Activities

### Qualitative Risk Assessment

- Asset identification
- Threat identification
- Vulnerability assessment
- Risk analysis
- Risk prioritization
- Risk treatment recommendations
- 5×5 Risk Matrix development



### Quantitative Risk Assessment

Financial risk calculations included:

- Single Loss Expectancy (SLE)
- Annualized Loss Expectancy (ALE)
- Cost-Benefit Analysis
- Return on Security Investment (ROSI)
